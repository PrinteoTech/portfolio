B.A.R.Y.O.N

A miniature tokamak for energy.

B.A.R.Y.O.N

The aim of the BARYON project is to design a miniature tokamak capable of producing enough energy to power a house using nuclear fusion. This involves the study of plasma physics, magnetic confinement, and the engineering challenges associated with miniaturizing such a device. The project is currently in its prototyping phase, where I am working on modeling and optimizing the reactor's efficiency to achieve stable plasma confinement. This project allows me to deepen my knowledge in nuclear physics, electromagnetism, and experimental engineering.

A.T.H.E.N.A

An AI evolving into an assistant.

A.T.H.E.N.A

A.T.H.E.N.A is an advanced artificial intelligence project inspired by J.A.R.V.I.S. from Iron Man. The goal is to create a multifunctional AI assistant capable of performing real-time data analysis, answering technical questions, and managing various tasks like scheduling and home automation. The AI utilizes deep learning models trained on technical documentation and real-world problem-solving scenarios. Future developments include voice synthesis, facial recognition, and integration with robotics for physical interaction.

VOXEL

AI-based voice cloning.

VOXEL

VOXEL is an AI-driven voice cloning system capable of reproducing natural and realistic speech patterns. The project involves training a deep learning model using Tacotron for text-to-speech conversion and WaveNet for high-fidelity audio generation. The objective is to develop an efficient and versatile TTS engine that can be integrated into various applications, such as virtual assistants, accessibility tools, and entertainment. Future enhancements include real-time voice modulation and multi-speaker adaptation.

GUSTAVE

Automated powdered drink dispenser.

GUSTAVE

GUSTAVE (Gestion Universelle des Savoureux Transferts Automatiques de Vitamines et Énergie) is an automated drink dispenser that precisely mixes powdered beverages with water. The system consists of a pump for water dispensing, a servo-controlled powder selector, and a magnetic stirrer for uniform mixing. The project involves hardware and software integration, with an Android app (developed using Kodular) for user-friendly control. Future plans include IoT capabilities for remote operation and personalized drink customization based on user preferences.
